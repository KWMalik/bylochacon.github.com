During the Holocaust much of Jewish cultural heritage was destroyed — religious objects melted down and books burned or sent for pulp. Only a sample of Jewish culture was preserved by the Nazis for their own ‘scientific’ purposes. At war’s end Allied forces uncovered huge stores of looted books, often lying strewn in makeshift depots. What was to be done with this valuable cultural legacy?
