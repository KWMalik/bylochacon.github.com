I have changed the Search for this site and moved it to the top menu, but it still doesn’t seem to be working.  I’m trying to get help repairing it, and after that will be left to repair is the RSS feed link.


I have added a Definitions of Killing page to help clear up the confusion between so many overlapping terms dealing with mass murder.  I will be adding to it and clarifying it for the future, and comments are appreciated.  Back in 2005, I made a handout on this topic for discussion in a class I was taking.  It includes examples of each, and if I’m correct, you can download it by clicking here:  Semantics of Mass Murder.pdf.


I have added recommendations from Pete Fredlake to the Bibliography of Ethnic Cleansing in the Former Yugoslavia and Links for Teacher Training & Organizations.


Kristyn Moravetz has recommended two books for the Bibliography of Human Rights & Women, and those have been the first additions to that list.


My excellent former T.A., David Armstead, is working on finding information to add to the information linked to the Hate topic page.


This weekend I was able to go Washington D.C. and touch bases with the director of the United States Holocaust Museum’s Teacher Fellowship program, Mr. Pete Fredlake, and get some help from the Smithsonian African Art Museum’s head librarian Dr. Janet Stanley.  Both are extremely kind and helpful, and I recommend both institutions for help with learning and teaching without reservation.
