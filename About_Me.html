ABOUT ME

 
 

I taught high school world history for eight years in Fremont, California, have a Bachelor of Arts in History from the University of California, Santa Barbara, and recently earned a Master of Arts in World History with an emphasis in teaching from San José State University.  I was selected as a teaching fellow at the United States Holocaust Memorial Museum for 2007-2008.  To study genocide and its related issues, I have traveled to Poland, the Czech Republic, Kenya and Rwanda.  Currently, I am studying, working on the board of Globaleyes, and taking care of my daughter Josie.  My goal is to enter a PhD program in the next couple years to further my studies.
